The Artistic Vision

Dr. Nikunj Gautham (PhD)

A distinguished PhD scholar in Music, Dr. Nikunj Gautham brings a wealth of knowledge and soulful expertise to the Riiyazii Music Academy. He has dedicated over 17+ years to intensive learning and Sadhana under the profound guidance of his Guru, mastering the intricate depths of Indian Classical tradition.

As an author, educator, and artist, he has dedicated his life to mentoring the next generation of musicians, ensuring that every student finds their unique voice and technical perfection.
